Why I Got Into Gunpla

Gundam has been a part of my life for years. It all started when I watched the Gundam anime "Mobile Fighter G Gundam", when it was airing on Toonami and was fascinated by the intricate designs of the mechas. I was drawn to the idea of building and customizing my own Gundam models. The craftsmanship and creativity involved in Gunpla builds are incredibly rewarding. It's not just about assembling parts, but creating something unique that reflects my personal interests. Every kit I build is a reminder of the connection between the hobby and the rich world of Gundam!
