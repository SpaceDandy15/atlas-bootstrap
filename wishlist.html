Fun Facts About Gunpla

Did you know? Gunpla stands for Gundam Plastic Model. The hobby has been popular since 1980, and over 500 million kits have been sold worldwide!

Gunpla Kits by Grade

There are various grades of Gunpla kits, each with different levels of detail and complexity. Here are the approximate numbers of kits made for each grade:
  • HG (High Grade): Over 200 kits
  • RG (Real Grade): Over 50 kits
  • MG (Master Grade): Over 100 kits
  • PG (Perfect Grade): Around 10 kits
  • SD (Super Deformed): Over 150 kits
These numbers are estimates and continue to grow as new Gunpla models are released.
